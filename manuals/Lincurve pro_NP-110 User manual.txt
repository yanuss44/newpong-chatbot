1 System overview 
1.1 System description 
LINCURVE PRO (NP-110) delivers concentrated ultrasound energy to the lower part of the skin 
through a focused ultrasound transducer. A small thermal coagulation zone is formed in the 
deepskin layers such as the dermis, the boundary layer between the dermis and subcutaneous fat, 
the SMAS layer, and the fascia. The area where ultrasound energy is concentrated causes 
coagulation of the tissue, and gradual thermal damage is created in nearby tissues, leading to 
various degrees of collagen degeneration. This process stimulates the tissue’s natural healing 
response, resulting in skin lifting, tightening, and improved elasticity. 
 
1.2 Intended use 
The LINCURVE PRO is used to achieve skin lifting and tightening effects by inducing the 
regeneration of collagen and elastic Fibers. This is accomplished when focused ultrasound energy 
selectively coagulates the subcutaneous and SMAS layers for the indications for use described 
below. 
•  Eyebrow and sagging chin lifting/tightening 
•  Improves firmness of skin on jaw line, peri-orbital, cheek, abdomen, thigh and arm 
•  Improve fine lines and wrinkles on the chest(decolletage) and neck area        
•  Improving cellulite and stretch marks

2 Safety Precautions 
2.1 Warnings and Precautions 
1) Read and understand the instructions before use. 
2) Only the authorized operator may use this device. 
3) The procedure must be performed by a doctor or licensed medical personnel under the 
direction and supervision of a doctor. 
4) Dispose of the main unit and cartridge in accordance with applicable regulations. For any 
issues related to disposal, please contact Newpong Co., Ltd. or your authorized distributor. 
 
2.2 General precautions 
1) Before use, be sure to read and understand this user manual, and use only the designated 
components. 
2) Doctors and all personnel involved in the procedure must be familiar with this user manual. 
3) If any issues occur during use, contact the manufacturer for assistance. 
4) Do not attempt to repair or modify the device unless you are a certified Newpong service 
technician. 
5) All maintenance and service must be performed by authorized technicians using approved 
components. 
6) Do not disassemble or modify the product under any circumstances. 
 
2.3 Maintenance precautions 
1) Clean the handpiece with alcohol to maintain hygiene. 
2) Store the cartridge at room temperature to prevent the internal liquid from freezing, which may 
cause damage. Do not use frozen cartridges. 
3) When cleaning the LCD touchscreen, use a soft cloth moistened with alcohol. 
4) To clean the surface of the device, wipe it with a soft cloth moistened with alcohol. Avoid 
allowing any liquid to enter the device. 
5) Ensure that the hose connecting the main unit and the handpiece is not bent or twisted. 
6) Do not use or store the device in areas with high humidity or poor ventilation. Avoid exposure to 
direct sunlight or high-temperature environments. 
 
2.4 Precautions during use 
1) Do not operate the device near flammable or combustible gases or materials. 
2) Check the coolant level inside the cartridge before use. Do not operate the device if the coolant 
is absent or insufficient.

10) To prevent electric shock, connect the device only to a power supply with protective grounding.

- Patients with skin allergies to local anesthetics 
- Pregnant women or infants 
- Patients with keloid-prone skin 
- Patients with pacemakers, stents, or metal implants 
- Patients with skin cancer 
2) Patients with the following conditions must consult with a medical specialist before proceeding 
with the treatment: 
- Pregnant or breastfeeding women

2.7 Procedure precautions 
1) Apply ultrasound gel to ensure close contact between the cartridge and the treatment area 
during the procedure. Failure to maintain proper contact may result in inaccurate focal depth, 
reduced treatment efficacy, or skin burns. Be careful not to allow air bubbles to form within the 
gel, as they may cause thermal damage to surrounding tissues. 
2) There is a risk of bubble formation along the acoustic path during treatment, particularly at the 
interface between the transducer and tissue. To avoid this, ensure the film surface of the 
cartridge is always facing downward during ultrasound emission. Additionally, air bubbles in 
the acoustic path can reduce treatment effectiveness. Therefore, only degassed water should 
be filled in the cartridge. 
3) The uniform propagation of High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) waves may be disrupted 
by bone or gas-containing organs in the treatment area. Use caution when treating areas with 
loose or sagging skin, as there is a risk of facial nerve injury.

4) Patients may experience pain or itching during the procedure. If such symptoms occur, follow 
the physician's instructions. 
5) The following temporary side effects may occur after treatment. In case of side effects, follow 
the instructions of a medical professional: 
- Numbness 
- Stiffness 
- Nerve paralysis 
- Erythema, itching, or swelling 
- Pain, bruising, erosion, blistering, or ulceration 
- Hypopigmentation or hyperpigmentation 
6) If abnormal symptoms such as skin depressions or excessive swelling occur, stop the treatment 
immediately. 
7) Do not apply treatment repeatedly to the same area in succession. 
8) Before treatment, clearly explain all possible side effects to the patient and obtain a signed 
informed consent form. 
9) When using the S2 cartridge, which emits single shots, avoid overlapping exposures on the 
same spot. Move continuously during treatment to prevent damage. 
 
2.8 Expected adverse events 
1) Unintended heating of tissues may occur, potentially resulting in skin damage or fistula 
formation. 
2) Thermal injury may affect the skin, surrounding tissues, or adjacent anatomical structures.

6.4.2 READY status 
 
1) After completing the settings, press the ‘READY’ button to enter the READY status. 
2) The ‘STOP’ button is only enabled in the READY status, and all other buttons (except 
STANDBY/STOP) are disabled. 
※ The ‘STOP’ button is used for emergency shutdown. If the ‘STOP’ button is pressed

status. 
3) Output will only begin when the shot button on the handpiece is pressed.
