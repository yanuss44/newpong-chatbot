2. General 
2.1. Introduction 
NP-200 of Newpong Co., Ltd. is an ultrasonic surgical unit designed to fragment and emulsify subcutaneous fatty 
tissues   using   ultrasound   frequency   vibrations.   The   NP-200  generates  ultrasonic  vibrations  through  the 
piezoelectric  effect  of  PZT  ceramic  crystals  embedded  in  the  NP-200  handpieces.  When  electrical  energy  is 
supplied from the main control unit, these PZT crystals rapidly expand and contract, converting electrical signals 
into mechanical ultrasonic vibrations. These vibrations are transmitted to the attached probe, which delivers the 
mechanical action required for tissue fragmentation and emulsification.  
The  NP-200  is  equipped  with  an  electronic  control  system  that  allows  the  operator  to  adjust  the  vibration 
amplitude and frequency. It offers two operating modes, Continuous Mode and Pulse Mode. 
 
2.2. Intended Use  
LSSA  is  intended  for  the  fragmentation  and  emulsification  of  subcutaneous  fatty  tissues  for  aesthetic  body  
contouring. LSSA is indicated for use in the following surgical specialists when the fragmentation and aspiration 
of adipose tissue is desired:  
- Plastic and Reconstruction Surgery  
- Orthopedic Surgery

3. Installation and Connection 
Please note following articles when “NP-200” is installed  
1) It is recommended that this device is installed at an airy or ventilated space because it has slight heat during 
operating.  
2) Be sure to use linear or grounded power supply to prevent against malfunctions by electrical effects.  
3) Do not try to get the power cord wet.  
4) Do not connect the power cord to main body if it is damaged.  
5) Do not share an outlet with other devices. There is potential for the fire by abnormal generated heat if 
several devices share an outlet.  
6) If possible, please avoid direct sunlight.  
7) Be careful not to damage by shock.  
8) Install the device at the flat location. 
9) Install the device away from dust or inflammables. 
10) To facilitate disconnection of the power supply in an emergency, install the equipment with a minimum 
distance of 15 cm between the rear of the equipment and the wall, and install it in a location where access 
to the equipment is not obstructed by obstacles or walls. 
11) Be careful not to block the exhaust vent as heat accumulates on the top and back of the equipment when 
the equipment is used continuously.

5. Cleaning and Maintenance 
 
5.1. Maintenance and storage 
5.1.1. Maintenance 
1) Always unplug the AC power cord and cables before cleaning the Main Body and handpiece. 
2) This Main Body, handpiece, probe and wrench must be kept clean and hygienic with proper care. 
3) Do not submerge the socket at the end of the handpiece in water. 
4) During cleaning, make sure to completely remove any moisture from the socket at the tip of the handpiece. 
Failure to do so may result in an electrical short. 
5) Immediately remove all impurities of outside the device and accessories after treatment.  
6) Do not break the handpiece cable.  
7) If the device has not been used for a long time, check firstly whether the device is working in safety.  
8) Avoid the environment that temperature can be increased by direct sunlight.  
9) Regular inspection for device and accessories is recommended. 
10) After clean the device, check the main body and accessories seriously. If the device is old or damaged, do 
not use the device. 
11) Remove the power of device before cleaning and maintenance. Otherwise, it may harm to people or 
damage the product. 
12) Some chemical cleaning products may have conductivity and make some scraps. The products remain with 
conductivity dusts or scraps. Do not use the products for electrical part and do not use spray cleaning 
products for any surfaces. 
 
5.1.2. Storage 
1) Separate a power cord from the outlet. 
2) Store the device avoiding a wet place. 
3) Be careful with the bad influence like air containing atmospheric pressure, temperature, humidity, 
ventilation, dust and salty.  
4) Be careful with incline, vibration and shock. 
5) Do not store the device in storage place for chemicals and place where generates gas. 
6) Store the device without power cord by using cover as like vinyl when the device is stored in a long time 
7) If the device has not been used over a month, check whether the device works in safety. 
8) Store with consideration of sanitation, sterilization before reuse of contact point with skin is recommended. 
9) Store this NP-200 in a clean, dry and non-corrosive atmosphere under the conditions below. 
- Temperature: 0  °C  to + 60  °C 
- Relative Humidity: 10 to 90 % (non-considering) 
- Atmospheric pressure: 800 to 1 060 hPa
