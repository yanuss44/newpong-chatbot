Lincurve Pro
NP-110
SERVICE MANUAL (Text-based Knowledge Base)

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
The Lincurve Pro (NP-110) is an advanced medical device specializing in ultrasonic treatments. It uses a high-frequency transducer to deliver focused energy.

2. SPECIFICATIONS
- Model: NP-110
- Frequency: 20kHz - 40kHz (Variable)
- Input: 100-240VAC, 50/60Hz
- Power: Max 150VA

3. ERROR CODES & TROUBLESHOOTING
Er 01: System Overheat
- Cause: Ventilation blocked or fan failure.
- Resolution: Check fan operation, clear vents, and wait for 10 minutes.

Er 05: Transducer Communication Error
- Cause: Handpiece cable not fully inserted.
- Resolution: Re-connect the handpiece and restart the device.

Er 12: High Voltage Fault
- Cause: Internal SMPS failure.
- Resolution: Turn off main power immediately and contact Newpong service.

4. MAINTENANCE
- Inspect probes every 100 treatments.
- Clean handpiece with 70% Isopropanol after each use.
- Do NOT submerge the main body in water.

(End of Manual)
